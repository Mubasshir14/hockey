Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers with our Frequently Asked Questions—your go-to resource for clear insights and helpful information about our services and offerings.

How can I join Hockey Havoc?

Our Junior Program is designed for aspiring young athletes aged 5 to 16, providing age-appropriate training, skill development, and a supportive environment to nurture their love for the game.

What coaching credentials do your staff members hold?

Our coaching staff consists of experienced and certified professionals with backgrounds in elite-level play and coaching certifications, ensuring top-notch guidance and mentorship.

Is there financial assistance available for program fees?

We understand the importance of accessibility. Our club offers financial assistance programs; please contact our administration for confidential discussions and potential support options.

What age groups do you cater to in your Junior Program?

Our Junior Program is designed for aspiring young athletes aged 5 to 16, providing age-appropriate training, skill development, and a supportive environment to nurture their love for the game.

How are teams formed in the Professional Program?

Team formation in the Professional Program is based on a combination of skill assessments, player preferences, and team dynamics to ensure a competitive and cohesive roster.

What safety measures are in place during practices and games?

Safety is our priority. We follow strict protocols, including certified trainers, first aid stations, and adherence to all recommended safety guidelines to ensure a secure environment for all participants.
